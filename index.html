About me

Well, hello there. I'm Mike Hovenier (23), from the Netherlands. In July 2022 I graduated at the University of Applied Sciences in Amsterdam - Communication and Multimedia Design (CMD) - Faculty Digital Media and Creative Industry in Amsterdam (HvA). Yes that's a whole mouth full. I consider myself as a Visual and/or UX Designer. I'm currently looking for a full time job in Visual and/or UX Design industry near Amsterdam, Haarlem or working at home from a distance. I am also open to other design related things within the subject.

My goal is learning new skills that I can add to my current library. Even if I may don't like it, I think it's a good thing to have a little understanding off everything. To improve myself as a well sharped designer. For me design is about finding the right solution to the problem. Making something pretty is one thing, but making something userfriendly should be the number one priority. Perfect design is invisible.
